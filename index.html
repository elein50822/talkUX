  • " TALK UX 2017 @ TAIPEI "

    A global network of UXers 20-22, Oct, 2017

About

Talk UX is a platform for you to access to great speakers, great content, some fun, interactive sessions and new tech . It’s hosted by Ladies that UX, an international community of creative, inspiring women who work in UX design. In 2017, Talk UX will be held in Taipei, Taiwan . We would love you to join us at this fantastic event.

Pre-Party

In Pre-party, You would meet UXers from diversified industries around the world. It would be the best time to talk and share with them about design skills and work experiences. In such a casual atmosphere, it encourages you to find your design mentor, business partners and clients easily.

Keynote and Paneles

Talk UX is a two-days, inclusive conference where UXers can learn, collaborate, and network. You’ll hear from fresh speakers who will share their expertise on everything from design to research to data and everything in between. Through it all, we’ll foster a positive atmosphere that encourages learning and interaction.

Workshop

Workshops enable people who eager to learn from different methods to take insight enter their workplace. Workshops host by designers who have used those methods succeed in their own working environment. We believe it’s the best occasion each one teach one through the process.

Speaker

ELAYNA SPRATLEY

co-founder and chief creative officer of Adaptive Path

Currently working on the IBM Watson Education team on Watson Classroom, Elayna and her team are creating an experience that allows K-12 educators to design personalized classroom experiences for their learners.

IRINA SPICAKA

co-founder and chief creative officer of Adaptive Path

Irina Spicaka is a Latvian-born Berlin-based User Experience and Interface Designer, Digital Media Artist, Curator and Educator working in the field since 2005. As a UX/UI Designer she is experienced in Research for MVP, Competitor Analysis, User Testing, Copywriting, Search Engine Optimisation, Art Direction, Branding, Print and Styleguide Design, UI Component and Kit Design for Apps, SaaS and Websites.

SUSANNE KREUZ

co-founder and chief creative officer of Adaptive Path

I am a UX designer from southern Germany focusing on user research, information architecture, concept and content. My wide experiences in many disciplines, job roles and companies made me a t-shaped person who loves to work in interdisciplinary teams located around the globe.

ANNA ABOVYAN

co-founder and chief creative officer of Adaptive Path

Could a good UX help save more lives? How to conduct UX in a research-challenging settings like hospitals? Specialized in utilizing #speechrecognition technology in the #medical field, Anna Abovyan from MModal will join us
