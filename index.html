about-desc

Pre-Party

In Pre-party, You would meet UXers from diversified industries around the world. It would be the best time to talk and share with them about design skills and work experiences. In such a casual atmosphere, it encourages you to find your design mentor, business partners and clients easily.

Keynote and Panels

Talk UX is a two-days, inclusive conference where UXers can learn, collaborate, and network. You’ll hear from fresh speakers who will share their expertise on everything from design to research to data and everything in between. Through it all, we’ll foster a positive atmosphere that encourages learning and interaction.

Workshop

Workshops enable people who eager to learn from different methods to take insight enter their workplace. Workshops host by designers who have used those methods succeed in their own working environment. We believe it’s the best occasion each one teach one through the process.
